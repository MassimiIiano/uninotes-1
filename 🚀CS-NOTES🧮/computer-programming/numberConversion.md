$$

$$
$$

$$
11011 = 1\cdot2^4+1\cdot2^3+0\cdot2^2+1\cdot2^1+1\cdot2^0 = 27

$$
100011110 = 1\cdot2^8+0+0+0+1\cdot2^4+1\cdot2^3+1\cdot2^2+1\cdot2^1+0\cdot2^0 = 286
$$
